About Us

Our sandwich shop is one of the most diverse and interesting sandwich shops around. We include flavors from all over the world and a variety of sandwiches that are sure to make your mouth water! We only use the freshest of ingredients and all sandwiches are prepared per order. We include flavors from the deep hills of Tuscany, the streets of Vietnam's bustling cities, the bars of the United States, the pubs of England, the beaches of Maine, the French Brasseries, and the bustling markets of the Middle East. The menu also will change depending on the season. Our sandwiches are always fresh and the toppings, seasoning, and meats are always locally sourced. We also bake our own warm bread, rolls, and tortillas daily. We hope you enjoy!
